## üïπÔ∏è How to Play

1. **Initialize:** Click the `ENGAGE` button to start the mission.
2. **Move:** Use your **Left/Right Arrow Keys** (Desktop) or **Tap the sides of the screen** (Mobile).
3. **Objective:** Survive 100 sectors. The speed increases every time you clear a sector.
4. **Enemies:** - **Void Blocks (Pink):** Standard hazards falling at high velocity.
   - **Seekers (Purple):** Advanced AI blocks that track your movement (Starts at Level 10).
